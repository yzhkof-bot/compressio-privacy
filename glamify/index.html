Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Glamify FX ("we", "our", or "the App") is a real-time effect camera and AI beauty enhancement application for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Camera Access

Glamify FX requires access to your device's camera to function. This access is used solely to:

  • Display the live camera feed with real-time effects and beauty filters
  • Capture photos and record videos with effects applied

All camera processing, including face landmark detection and hand gesture tracking, is performed entirely on your device using on-device AI (MediaPipe). No camera data, facial data, or biometric information is ever transmitted to any server.

On-Device AI Processing

Glamify FX uses on-device machine learning for:

  • Face landmark detection — to apply beauty effects (skin smoothing, eye enlargement, face slimming, etc.)
  • Hand landmark tracking — to enable gesture-interactive effects (21 hand landmarks tracked in real time)

All AI processing runs locally on your device's GPU via Metal and WebGPU. No biometric data is collected, stored, or transmitted.

Information We Collect

We collect limited data through third-party services to improve the App:

  • Crash data and performance data — collected via Sentry for app stability monitoring. This data is not linked to your identity.
  • Advertising data and device identifiers (IDFA) — collected via Google AdMob to display ads. This data may be used for third-party advertising and tracking purposes.

Advertising

Glamify FX displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings. Glamify FX Pro subscribers enjoy a completely ad-free experience.

Subscription Information

Glamify FX offers optional in-app subscriptions (Weekly, Monthly, and Yearly plans) through Apple's App Store. All subscription transactions are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles your purchase data.

Cloud-Delivered Effects

Effect packages are downloaded from our CDN servers. This process does not collect or transmit any personal information. Only the effect package files are downloaded to your device.

Data Storage

Glamify FX stores minimal app preferences and settings locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. Photos and videos you capture are saved to your device's photo library. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

Glamify FX does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The crash and performance data we collect via Sentry is not linked to any user identity.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
