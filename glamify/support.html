Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use beauty effects?

Open the App and grant camera permission. Tap the beauty icon to access 8 adjustable beauty tools: skin smoothing, brightening, lipstick, teeth whitening, eye enlargement, face slimming, V-face, and nose refinement. Drag the slider to adjust intensity in real time.

How do I apply creative effects?

Tap the effects icon to browse the effect library. Select any effect to apply it to your live camera feed. Some effects are interactive — touch the screen or use hand gestures to interact with them.

How do I capture photos and videos?

With any effect or beauty filter active, tap the capture button to take a photo, or long-press to record a video. What you see on screen is exactly what gets saved.

Why do some effects require downloading?

Effects are delivered as cloud packages to keep the app lightweight. Each effect is downloaded once and cached on your device for future use.

What is Glamify FX Pro?

Pro unlocks unlimited photo and video capture, watermark-free exports, early access to new effects, and a completely ad-free experience. Choose from Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly subscription plans.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone. Find Glamify FX and tap to manage or cancel your subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All face and hand detection runs entirely on your device. No biometric data is transmitted to any server. Photos and videos are saved locally to your photo library. For details, see our Privacy Policy.
