Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Compressio ("we", "our", or "the App") is a video compression application for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Do Not Collect

Compressio is designed with privacy as a core principle. We want to be transparent about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not access, upload, or transmit your videos or photos to any server
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not use cookies or similar technologies
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Photo Library Access

Compressio requires access to your device's photo library to function. This access is used solely to:

  • Display your videos within the App for compression
  • Save compressed videos back to your photo library

All video processing is performed entirely on your device. Your videos never leave your device and are never uploaded to any external server.

Subscription Information

Compressio offers optional in-app subscriptions through Apple's App Store. All subscription transactions are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles your purchase data.

Data Storage

Compressio stores minimal app preferences (such as compression history and settings) locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

Compressio does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any user, we are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
